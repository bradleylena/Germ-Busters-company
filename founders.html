Founding Story

Founding Story Germ Busters was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that everyone deserves a clean, healthy space without the stress of doing it themselves. In the early days, Bradley Lenaiyarra started the company with just a few tools, a strong work ethic, and a commitment to quality service. As word spread about the care and consistency of his work, Germ Busters quickly grew into a trusted name in residential and commercial cleaning. Today, we proudly serve a wide range of clients while staying true to the principles we were founded on.

Founders Bio

Bradley Lenaiyarra is the heart behind Germ Busters. With years of hands-on experience in the cleaning industry, Bradley brings an unmatched attention to detail and a deep sense of pride in every job done. His leadership is built on honesty, reliability, and a customer-first approach that sets the tone for the entire team. He believes that cleaning isn't just about appearances—it’s about creating environments where people feel safe, comfortable, and cared for.
